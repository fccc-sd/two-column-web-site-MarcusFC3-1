    • Fitness

    While I can say that while fitness has positively impacted almost every part of my life, It was very difficult to start. I've found that fitness gives me plenty of intangible benefits upon consistent performance like higher levels of confidence and just feeling satisfied with myself overall. I also found that fitness can be made into something enjoyable instead of monotonously performing exercises and seeing the challenge as a downside, measuring my progress and striving to break my own records gives my workouts a sense of purpose that motivates me.

    Sports

    My top 3 sports are basketball, baseball, and football. Because I'm from Michigan, I'm always rooting for Detroit in every sport. Currently I am excited to watch the Detroit lions during the 2023-2024 NFL season because they may actually have a shot at a deep playoff run this year. I don't watch too much baseball because it isn't as fun to watch as other sports but going to baseball games in person and playing baseball are both really fun. Basketball might be my favorite sport of the three because I have played and watched it for the longest. While I always want detroit to win whenever I'm watching, I don't really follow a single team when watching basketball. The only sport I have ever officially participated in was track and field at Tinora High School as a long distance runner and while I did enjoy my time on the team, I prefer to run alone.
